# Research Literature

Use this skill when a workflow is framing a research topic, paper outline, or related
work plan.

Output:

- research question
- motivation and gap
- keyword groups for search
- paper buckets to collect
- baseline methods
- expected data and evaluation
- risks and validation plan

Rules:

- Keep the topic narrow enough to become an executable modeling or experiment plan.
- Prefer verifiable claims and measurable evaluation criteria.
- Separate background survey from methods that will actually be implemented.
